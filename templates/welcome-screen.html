We are devoting a lot of our time to build high quality modules and contribute to the Foundry community. Presently, this amounts to many hours a day. We recognize that not everyone has the resources to do so – which is fine! – but if you can safely afford that, please consider becoming a Patron and supporting module development. All contributions help, regardless of level.  In return we offer early access to new modules and major updates, discord roles, patron-only voting power and more.
